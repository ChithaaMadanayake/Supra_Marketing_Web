Supra MK5

The Toyota GR Supra (MK5) is a high-performance, rear-wheel-drive sports coupe, known for its dynamic handling, powerful engine, and track-inspired design, offering a blend of performance and luxury.

Key Features:
Performance: 3.0L turbocharged engine, 382 hp, 0-60 mph in 4.2 seconds.
Design: Sleek exterior, driver-focused interior, and advanced tech features.
Safety: Forward Collision Warning, Lane Departure Warning, and more.

Supra MK4

The Toyota Supra MK4 (1993-2002), is a legendary sports car renowned for its powerful 2JZ engine, sleek design, and performance capabilities.

Key Features:
Engine: 3.0L inline-6 2JZ engine with twin-turbo options.
Performance: 320 hp, 0-100 km/h in 4.9 seconds.
Tuning: Known for incredible tuning potential, making it a cultural icon.
The MK4 Supra also became a favorite in the import tuning scene and continues to be a sought-after classic. Its performance and legendary status have made it a staple in the world of street racing.

Supra A80

The Toyota Supra A80, is a legendary sports car known for its powerful 2JZ engine, available in both naturally aspirated and twin-turbo versions, and its iconic design.
Key Features:
Engine: 2JZ-GE and 2JZ-GTE .
Performance: 320 hp, 0-60 mph in 5.2 seconds.
Design: Aggressive lines, iconic rear wing, and a prominent silhouette.
Its cultural impact has been cemented by appearances in films like "Fast & Furious," where it became synonymous with high-performance and car culture. It's also one of the most customizable cars for performance enthusiasts.
